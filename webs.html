LGBTQ+++

is an acronym that stands for lesbian, gay, bisexual, transgender, and queer. It is used as a collective term to describe individuals who identify with a sexual orientation or gender identity that is not heterosexual or cisgender. The acronym is often used in conjunction with a plus sign (+) to signify that it encompasses a broader range of identities beyond the letters themselves.
